Why we build

A community that has outgrown its space

Mountain House has grown from a small planned community into one of the fastest-growing cities in California. Our community needs a permanent home — a masjid that serves as a center for prayer, learning, and gathering for generations to come.

Our programs

Serving every stage of life

From early childhood through adulthood, MHMA offers programs that nurture faith, community, and lifelong learning.

📚
Maktab
Weekend Islamic school covering Quran, Arabic, and Islamic studies for children ages 5–16.
🏀
MHMA Hoops
Youth basketball program building teamwork, discipline, and community for young Muslims.
🌿
Little Explorers Montessori
Early childhood program rooted in Islamic values and a spirit of wonder.
🥀
Iqraa Arabic Academy
Arabic language academy for all ages, from beginners through Quranic fluency.
💌
Women’s Committee
Events, circles, and initiatives led by and for the women of MHMA.
⭐
Quran Quest
Annual competition celebrating memorization and recitation excellence.

3. Masjid Construction Campaign Page
This is the single most important deliverable. NuecesMosque.com/reconstruction is the gold
standard. MHMA needs a dedicated, standalone campaign page — not just a section within an
existing page — that serves as the hub for all construction fundraising activity.

3.2 Required Components
Hero Section
• Full-width architectural rendering or site plan image of the future Islamic Center
• Bold headline: e.g., &#39;Build the Islamic Center of Mountain House&#39;
• Campaign tagline, e.g., &#39;Whoever builds a masjid for the sake of Allah...&#39;
• Two CTAs: [Donate Now] and [Pledge to Give]
Live Fundraising Progress Bar
• Display: Amount Raised / Total Goal ($8.5M)
• Animated progress bar, updated at least weekly (manual update is fine initially)
• Donor count or number of families who have contributed
• Reference: NuecesMosque.com shows this prominently
Project Overview Section
• Brief narrative: Why we need a masjid, community growth in Mountain House
• Key stats: estimated sq footage, projected capacity, community impact numbers
• Embed or link to project brochure PDF
• Embed a vision/campaign video if available (YouTube embed)
Giving Tiers / Campaign Milestones
• Display giving tiers with named levels (e.g., Platinum: $50K+, Gold: $25K+)
• Show construction milestones: Phase 1 Foundation = $X, Phase 2 Structure = $Y
• VRIC uses an &#39;Ansar Club&#39; monthly sustainer model — consider MHMA equivalent
Multiple Giving Options
• Online card via Stripe (already in use — keep)
• Zelle (with designated email, e.g., build@mhma.us or treasurer@mhma.us)
• Check payable to MHMA — show mailing address
• Employer matching / Benevity information
• Crypto donation option (optional Phase 2)
• Waqf / endowment giving note if applicable
Pledge System
• Add a [Pledge to Give] button — links to a simple Google Form or embedded form

• Pledge form fields: Name, Email, Amount, Timeframe (e.g., within 30/60/90 days)
• Pledges collected to separate tracking list; board can follow up
• Display: &#39;Not ready to donate? Pledge now and we&#39;ll remind you.&#39;
FAQ Section
• Model after NuecesMosque.com FAQ — accordion/expandable format
• Sample questions: What is the project vision? What does the building include? Are donations
tax-deductible? Is Zakat eligible? When does construction start? How are funds managed?
• Include MHMA 501(c)(3) EIN number for donor receipts
Testimonials / Community Voices
• 3–5 short community member quotes about why the masjid matters
